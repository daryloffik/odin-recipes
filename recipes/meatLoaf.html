MeatLoaf

Description

This moist meatloaf is a family recipe that is a big favorite at our house. By using
fresh bread instead of dried bread crumbs, you get a meatloaf that is juicy and
delicious.

Ingredient

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • ¾ cup small chunks of bread
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon sage
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ cup ketchup, or to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    Mix together ground beef, onion, bread, beaten eggs, milk, bell pepper, salt,
    sage, and black pepper in a large bowl until well combined. Press into a 9x5-inch
    loaf pan. Spread ketchup over the top of loaf.

  3. Step 3

    Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 45 minutes.
    An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160
    degrees F (70 degrees C).
