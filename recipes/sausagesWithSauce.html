sausagesWithSauce

Description

crockpot mac and cheese recipe is creamy, comforting, and takes just
moments to assemble in a slow cooker. Great for large family gatherings and to
take to potluck dinners. It's always a big hit!

Ingredient

  • 8 (4 ounce) links fresh Italian sausage
  • 1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise (Optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Gather all ingredients. .

  2. Step 2

    Place Italian sausage links, spaghetti sauce, green pepper, and onion into a slow
    cooker; mix until well combined.

  3. Step 3

    Cover and cook on Low for 6 hours. Serve on hoagie rolls.
